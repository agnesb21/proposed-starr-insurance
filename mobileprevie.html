📌 Mobile-Specific Dev Notes

Plan Cards — Swipeable On mobile, the comparison table becomes horizontally swipeable plan cards. Each card shows key coverage tiers at a glance. Touch-friendly tap targets (min 44px height on all buttons).
Quote Form — 2-Col Grid Fields reflow into a 2-column grid on mobile (vs. single row on desktop). "Get My Quote" becomes a full-width primary button with stronger visual weight.
Promo Banner — Stacked On mobile, the promo code and countdown stack vertically instead of side-by-side for legibility. Timer remains live via JS.
Nudge Banner — Compact Nudge text is shortened for mobile. CTA becomes full-width. Dismiss link stays as text to keep button area clean.
Support Cards — H-Scroll The three "How We Support You" cards scroll horizontally on mobile, each 150px wide, to preserve card detail without stacking too tall.
Soft Lock Overlay The fade + unlock prompt sits at the bottom of the plan cards area. On form submit, overlay removes and "Select Plan" buttons activate (JS toggle, no reload).
